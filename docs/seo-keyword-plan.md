# SEO + Google Ads Keyword Plan — Lead-Gen Terms

These are the **client-intent** phrases from the keyword list. They do *not* belong in
the Google News RSS feed (news outlets don't write "lawyer/sue/how-to" headlines, so
those queries return little). They belong here: as **SEO landing pages** and **paid-search
ad groups** that capture people actively looking for help.

All terms are **bottom-of-funnel / high-intent** — someone typing "data center property
damage lawyer" is far closer to calling than someone reading a news article. Prioritize
these for dedicated pages and (optionally) Google Ads.

> **TX attorney-advertising note:** Any of these landing pages is lawyer advertising under
> Texas Disciplinary Rules of Professional Conduct (Rule 7). Keep the site's disclaimer,
> avoid outcome guarantees / superlatives, label as advertising where required, and have
> the firm review page copy before launch. "Free, confidential review" is fine.

## Content clusters → landing pages

Each cluster = one focused landing page (better than one page targeting everything).
Suggested URL, H1, and the keywords it should rank for.

### 1. Data-center noise / nuisance
- **URL:** `/help/data-center-noise` (or fold into existing `/damage/air` + a CTA page)
- **H1:** "Data Center Noise Ruining Your Property? Know Your Options."

- **Secondary:** sue data center lawyer, crypto mine noise lawyer, Granbury crypto mine noise lawyer
- **Angle:** What nuisance law covers, the 24/7 hum/health complaints, what documentation helps (decibel logs, medical notes), free review CTA.

### 2. Data-center water (depletion + contamination)

### 4. Transmission lines / eminent domain / easements *(NEW vertical — Oncor / CenterPoint)*

## Google Ads — ad-group structure

Group tightly so ad copy matches the search. Use **phrase** and **exact** match (broad match
wastes budget on these). Start small, expand on what converts.

| Ad group | Keywords (phrase/exact) |
|---|---|
| DC – Noise | "data center noise nuisance attorney", "sue data center lawyer", "crypto mine noise lawyer" |
| DC – Water | "data center well water depletion", "sue data center water contamination", "water rights lawyer Texas data center", "aquifer drain data center Texas" |
| DC – Property | "data center property damage lawyer", "property value drop data center", "transmission tower property damage" |
| Transmission/Eminent Domain | "Oncor eminent domain lawyer Texas", "fight transmission line easement", "CenterPoint power line land grab", "forced utility easement Texas", "how to fight Oncor land offer" |
| Litigation | "class action data center Texas", "TCEQ data center permit violation" |
| Geo – Granbury | "Granbury crypto mine noise lawyer", "Hood County data center lawsuit" |
| Geo – Sulphur Springs | "Sulphur Springs MSB Global lawsuit", "Hopkins County Oncor easement" |

**Suggested negatives:** jobs, careers, salary, stock, investment, REIT, build, construction
contractor, internship — strip out boosterism and job-seeker traffic.

**Each ad group → its matching landing page (above), not the homepage.** Match scent:
the "noise" ad lands on the noise page. This lifts Quality Score and conversion rate.

1. Build clusters **1–3** first (core data-center harm; on-brand, lowest lift).
2. Add **cluster 6** geo pages for current hotspots (cheap, high-converting long-tail).
