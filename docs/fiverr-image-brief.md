# Fiverr Image Brief — Stock Photo Sourcing

Paste-ready brief for a freelancer sourcing **real, commercial-use-licensed stock photos**
(not AI-generated). This is the brief used for the Fiverr stock-photo gig.

> **Note:** Order is **50 photos**, same 7 categories (rescaled down from an original ~250 target).
> (This is distinct from `IMAGE-SHOTLIST.md`, which is the AI-generated hero/banner shotlist.)

---

## Paste-ready message

**Image brief — Big Data Damage (Texas data center / land & water site)**

I need **real, commercial-use-licensed stock photos** for a Texas website about data centers
and their impact on land and water. Realistic photos only — **no AI-generated images.**

**Specs:** horizontal/landscape, min 1200px wide, `.jpg`, Texas-relevant where possible,
warm golden-hour lighting preferred.

**Categories (mix across all; rough counts for 50 total):**

1. **Data centers & infrastructure (~6)** — large data-center buildings/exteriors, server halls,
   construction sites, cooling towers, industrial campuses (day & lit-up at night), power
   substations, transmission lines, cranes.

2. **Water (~11 — biggest theme)** — water wells, Texas-style water-pumping windmills, well pumps;
   drought (cracked dry earth, low/empty reservoirs, dry riverbeds, withered crops); healthy water
   (rivers, springs, creeks — Hill Country style); irrigation (center-pivot sprinklers); tap water,
   faucets, a glass of water, water-test kits, murky/contaminated water; livestock at water troughs.

3. **Air & pollution (~5)** — industrial smokestacks emitting, rows of diesel backup generators,
   smog/haze over a landscape, factory exhaust, hazy sky over homes.

4. **Property, homes & value (~8)** — rural Texas farmhouses & ranch homes, "For Sale" signs in
   yards, homes with acreage, fences/gates/property lines, foundation cracks & structural damage,
   industrial sites looming next to neighborhoods.

5. **Texas land & landscape (~9)** — ranch land, Panhandle plains, farmland (cotton/corn/wheat),
   cattle ranching, rural roads & big-sky scenes, windmills on the plains.

6. **Legal, rights & accountability (~6)** — permit documents & paperwork, public meetings /
   town-hall hearings, people reviewing documents, county courthouses, the Texas Capitol, a
   handshake/signing. (Use gavels/scales sparingly — they read as cliché.)

7. **People & landowners (~5)** — farmers & ranchers working the land, worried homeowners/families
   on porches, people inspecting wells or water, neighbors talking, hands holding soil or water.

**LICENSING (hard requirement):** Only images that are **royalty-free / Creative Commons CC0 /
cleared for commercial website use** (e.g. Pixabay, Unsplash, Pexels, Wikimedia CC0). For **each**
image, include the **source URL + license**. No Google-scraped or unlicensed images — this is for a
public commercial site and licensing must be clean.

## When photos arrive
Drop the folder anywhere in the project; the images get sorted, renamed, assigned to the right
articles, wired into frontmatter (`image:`), and deployed. Extras get stashed as a local library
the content pipeline can pull from later.
