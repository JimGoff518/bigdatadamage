# BigDataDamage — Content Calendar & Topic Plan

_Last updated: 2026-06-16. Living doc — update the Status column as pieces ship._

## Strategy in one paragraph

The site already has strong **water** coverage and decent **property** coverage. The two
biggest, cheapest opportunities are (1) **geo gap-fill** — 10 of 15 location pages have no
supporting article, and local "[city] data center [harm]" queries are low-competition and
high-intent — and (2) **air**, which is underbuilt (only 2 pieces). Every geo article is
anchored to a *different aquifer/region*, so they differentiate naturally and don't cannibalize.
Target cadence: **8 articles/month (≈2/week)** with human review before deploy. Quality over a
daily firehose — on a YMYL niche, a templated daily stream gets demoted.

## Topic clusters (pillars → spokes)

- **Water / "Stolen Water"** (pillar: `/damage/water`) — aquifer depletion, Rule of Capture,
  subsidence, permits, contamination. _Mature; extend mainly via geo + process spokes._
- **Air / "Poisoned Air"** (pillar: `/damage/air`) — diesel generators, TCEQ permits, health.
  _Underbuilt — priority._
- **Property / "Ruined Value"** (pillar: `/damage/property`) — noise/nuisance, diminution,
  selling near a facility. _Solid; extend via process + geo._
- **Your Rights / Legal Process** (pillar: `/your-rights`) — evidence, claims, limitations,
  finding help. _Conversion-supporting connective tissue across all harms._

## Anti-cannibalization guardrails

- Before drafting, check the new target keyword against existing titles in this doc + the
  articles folder (later: Pinecone semantic check). If overlap > ~70%, re-angle or merge.
- Geo water pieces **must** lead with the local aquifer/region specifics, not generic gallons
  figures (those live in `data-center-water-cooling-projections-2026`).
- One canonical page per intent. New "how much / what is" explainers route to the existing
  pillar, not a near-duplicate.
